01 Our Story

A modern firm, built around client outcomes.

We founded 24 Law Chambers on a single conviction: that legal counsel should be measured not by how complicated it can sound, but by how clearly it moves a client forward. Over the years, that conviction has shaped a firm whose lawyers and consultants come from different backgrounds — commerce, regulation, finance, technology, the academy — and bring their differences to bear on every matter.

The result is counsel that is both rigorous and practical. We are easy-going and open-minded, treating every client with enthusiasm, respect, and ethics — believing business can be both professional and fun.

Lagos · 29 Shakiru Anjorin
02 Our Approach

We listen first.

Each engagement begins by understanding the commercial outcome our client is trying to reach — and then designing the legal pathway. Hierarchy follows expertise, not the other way around. Junior lawyers run point on matters where their training fits best; senior partners step in where the stakes demand.

We are unafraid of complexity, but allergic to opacity. The advice we give is the advice we’d want to receive.

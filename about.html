Senior counsel for complex Nigerian and cross-border problems.

24 Law Chambers is a Nigerian law firm providing senior strategic legal counsel for high-value public-sector recovery, energy and infrastructure disputes, regulatory enforcement, arbitration, and Nigeria–UK cross-border legal coordination.

01 The Firm

Led by Emonye Olayinka Adekwu, SAN, FCIArb.

24 Law Chambers is led by Emonye Olayinka Adekwu, SAN, FCIArb — a Senior Advocate of Nigeria, Fellow of the Chartered Institute of Arbitrators, and Barrister of England and Wales. The Firm is built for matters where legal strategy, senior judgment, institutional credibility, and execution matter.

We support clients in high-value disputes, public-sector recovery, energy and infrastructure mandates, regulatory enforcement, arbitration, litigation, debt recovery, insolvency, government interface, and cross-border Nigerian legal issues.

Public Revenue Recovery & Government Mandates

ii.

Energy, Power & Infrastructure

iii.

iv.

v.

Corporate, Commercial & Transactional Advisory

vii.

Nigeria–UK Cross-Border Counsel

Supporting UK law firms, international counsel, investors, Nigerian businesses, families, and high-net-worth clients with Nigeria-facing legal matters and coordinated cross-border strategy.

Private Client, Family & Estate

Family, estate, property, inheritance, matrimonial, and private-client matters involving Nigerian and UK interests.

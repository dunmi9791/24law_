01 Why 24 Law

A flat firm with a steep learning curve.

From your first month, you’ll work alongside the Managing Partner on real matters — not as an extra body, but as a contributor whose research, drafting and judgment are part of the file. We invest in our lawyers’ development through structured mentorship, formal training, and the kind of unstructured proximity to senior counsel that good firms have always relied on.

We also believe — and we mean this — that work-life balance is not a slogan. We expect excellence; we don’t expect martyrdom.
